# Privacy Policy for ChatRPG

**Last Updated: 2025-12-19**

## 1. Introduction

ChatRPG ("the Application") is a proprietary, free-to-use Model Context Protocol (MCP) server designed for personal tabletop gaming. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the Application.

## 2. Infrastructure and Hosting

The Application is hosted on **Railway** (Railway.com).

- **Server Processing**: All game logic and data processing occur on servers provided by Railway.
- **Data Storage**: Game data (characters, campaigns) may be stored on the server's persistent storage volumes.
- **Access**: While the Application runs on cloud infrastructure, the Developer does not actively monitor, harvest, or sell individual user sessions or game data.

## 3. Interaction with Third-Party AI Providers

The Application functions as a bridge to Large Language Models (LLMs) such as OpenAI's ChatGPT.

- **Data Flow**: Your inputs are sent to the Application server (on Railway), processed, and forwarded to the AI provider (e.g., OpenAI) to generate responses.
- **Provider Policies**: Your interactions are subject to the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service of the AI provider you are using.

## 4. No Commercial Tracking

- We do not use cookies, trackers, or analytics.
- We do not sell user data.
- We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

## 5. Contact

For questions regarding this Application, please contact the Developer via the GitHub repository: https://github.com/Mnehmos/rpg-lite-mcp
